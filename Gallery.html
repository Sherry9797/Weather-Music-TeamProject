Discover awesome restaurants

Toronto

  • Buca:
    Italian restaurant offers delicious house-made pasta, wood-fired pizza, and a great selection of wines.
  • Lee:
    Susur Lee’s flagship restaurant offers an eclectic menu of Asian fusion cuisine, with dishes like Singaporean-style slaw and Singaporean-style chili lobster.
  • Canoe:
    Located on the 54th floor of the TD Bank Tower, this restaurant offers stunning views of the city skyline along with a menu of Canadian cuisine, including dishes like venison and Arctic char.
  • Pai:
    This popular Thai restaurant serves up delicious dishes like pad Thai and green curry, as well as some lesser-known but equally delicious options like khao soi and laab
  • Richmond Station:
    This cozy restaurant offers seasonal Canadian cuisine, with a focus on local and sustainable ingredients. Their house-made charcuterie is not to be missed!

    • Vancouver

  • Miku:
    This Japanese restaurant offers delicious sushi and sashimi, as well as a unique style of sushi called aburi, which is lightly seared with a blowtorch for added flavor.
  • Hawksworth:
    This upscale restaurant offers contemporary Canadian cuisine, with dishes like Pacific halibut and Alberta beef tenderloin.
  • The Fish Counter:
    This seafood market and restaurant offers sustainable and locally sourced seafood, with options like fish and chips and lobster rolls.
  • MeeT:
    This vegan restaurant offers delicious plant-based options like burgers, bowls, and poutine.
  • Tojo's:
    This sushi restaurant is run by Tojo, a legendary chef who is credited with inventing the California roll. Be sure to try the omakase, a chef's choice tasting menu.

    • Ottawa

  • Atelier:
    This unique restaurant offers a multi-course tasting menu that showcases the chef's creativity and culinary skill.
  • Beckta Dining & Wine:
    This upscale restaurant offers a contemporary Canadian menu, with dishes like Quebec venison and PEI mussels.
  • El Camino:
    This trendy restaurant offers delicious Mexican-inspired dishes like tacos, ceviche, and grilled octopus.
  • Play Food & Wine:
    This small plates restaurant offers a wide variety of dishes, with a focus on local and seasonal ingredients.
  • Fauna:
    This farm-to-table restaurant offers innovative Canadian cuisine, with dishes like roasted parsnip agnolotti and Nova Scotia lobster.

    • Calgary

  • Model Milk:
    This trendy restaurant offers modern Canadian cuisine, with dishes like Alberta beef tartare and pork belly.
  • River Café:
    This beautiful restaurant is located in Prince's Island Park and offers a menu of Canadian cuisine, with dishes like bison carpaccio and wild mushroom risotto.
  • Anju:
    This Korean-inspired restaurant offers delicious dishes like fried chicken and kimchi stew, as well as a great selection of cocktails.
  • Pigeonhole:
    This small plates restaurant offers a creative and innovative menu, with dishes like smoked steelhead trout and roasted lamb shoulder.
  • Shokunin:
    This Japanese restaurant offers delicious sushi, sashimi, and yakitori, as well as a great selection of sake.
